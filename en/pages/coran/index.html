القُرْآن

The Quran

The Recited Book

Each sūra in its Arabic text — its translation, its commentary
verse by verse, and the analysis of every word.

This module opens the Quran slowly, sūra by sūra. The vocalised Arabic text, a translation, a commentary for each verse, and — by touching a word — its root and its grammatical construction.

The aim is not to give everything at once, but to inhabit the text: to read it, to understand it, to meditate on it. The module grows from the end of the Quran — the shortest sūras first.
