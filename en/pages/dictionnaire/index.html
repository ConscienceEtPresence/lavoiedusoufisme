About This dictionary draws principally on the Encyclopedia of the technical terms of Islamic Sufism (Mawsūʿat muṣṭalaḥāt at-taṣawwuf al-islāmī) by Dr Rafīq al-ʿAjam (Beirut, Maktabat Lubnān Nāshirūn, 1999, 1204 pages) — the major contemporary reference in the Arabic language. The definitions are synthesised, completed by other sources (Chittick, al-Qāshānī, al-Qushayrī…), and enriched with questions for meditation.

208 terms at present — the base will grow progressively to cover, in time, the essential core of the Sufi vocabulary.
