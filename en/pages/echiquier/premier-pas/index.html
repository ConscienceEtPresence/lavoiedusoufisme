الخطوة الأولى

First step

Never read anything about Sufism? Start here. Seven squares only, in order, like a little story: a threshold, a slope, a fall, a return, a help, a term.

How I feel First step How to use it Full board (FR)

Don't look for “your square”. Simply read, in order, and let each image resonate. At each step, a link opens the full square on the board if you wish to go deeper. Nothing to succeed at, nothing to judge.

✦
  1. 1

    العدم

    al-ʿadam — non-being

    At the start, nothing. Before existing, we were not. All that we are was given to us. The path begins with this humility: I did not make myself.

    Open square 1 →
  2. 2

    الشهوة

    al-shahwa — passion

    Then comes desire. It is not bad in itself: it is the engine of life. But if it becomes the master, it drags downward. It is the first slope, where the soul (النفس, nafs) takes the controls.

    Open square 4 →
  3. 3

    الصحبة الردية

    al-ṣuḥba al-radiyya — bad company

    A typical fall: one lets oneself be surrounded by what pulls downward. The commentary says a “grapnel” then seizes the traveller and makes him descend several degrees. A fall is not a condemnation — it is a call to return.

    Open square 30 →
  4. 4

    الصحبة الطيبة

    al-ṣuḥba al-ṭayyiba — good company

    The remedy is often the exact reversal of the fall. To choose a company that raises: “a man will be raised in the religion of his friend.” This single square can make one cross, in one step, twenty-four degrees toward good works.

    Open square 40 →
  5. 5

    ترحم العريان

    al-taraḥḥum al-ʿuryān — compassion for the naked man

    A virtue that raises: tenderness for the one we see destitute. The “naked man” designates the essential indigence of every being. From this compassion an ascent toward good character is born.

    Open square 39 →
  6. 6

    المرشد الكامل

    al-murshid al-kāmil — the perfect guide

    No one walks alone. The guide (شيخ, shaykh) welcomes the traveller: “Be welcome, I have long awaited you,” and leads him, in a single step, toward the term.

    Open square 62 →
  7. 7

    بقاء بالله

    al-baqāʾ bi-llāh — permanence in God

    The term of the journey: what remains when the self ceases to claim itself. But even here, the guide warns against pride: the highest station remains that of servanthood (العبودية, ʿubūdiyya).

    Open square 96 →
