The way of wisdom · Section 2

كتاب الحكم

The Aphorisms

300 brief aphorisms, to be reread across a lifetime. Choose your path of reading: walk through the book as it was written, or enter by a theme that calls to you today.

I

Read by chapter

Follow the book in its order: 24 chapters, the 4 letters and the Munājāt. At your own pace, as one turns the pages.
II

Read by theme

Enter by what dwells in you — trust, speech, weariness, return, gratitude. The aphorisms of the Ḥikam gathered around a single watchfulness.

Text in English, original rendering. Pedagogical commentary in English. The vocalised Arabic will be added later, after source verification.
