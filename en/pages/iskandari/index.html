طريق الحكمة

The way of wisdom

with Ibn ʿAṭāʾ Allāh al-Iskandarī · d. 709 H / 1309

A companionship seven centuries old. Third master of the Shādhilī way, Ibn ʿAṭāʾ Allāh leaves behind him brief aphorisms, two treatises, and intimate prayers. This section gathers them into five doors, each with its own reading, each with its own pace.

✦

المؤلف وأعماله

The author and his works

Who is Ibn ʿAṭāʾ Allāh al-Iskandarī? The Shādhilī transmission, his context, and the three works he left behind.

Introduction
2

كتاب الحكم

The Aphorisms

The central book — 300 brief aphorisms, each one to be reread a whole life long. To be read chapter by chapter, in the order of the book, or entered through a theme that calls to you today.

Reading room · 300 aphorisms
3

المناجاة

The Munājāt — intimate prayers

At the foot of the book: 38 prayers addressed to God, brief, with no pleading. What the heart says when it no longer has to defend itself — when it only recognises.

Intimate prayers
4

الكتابان

The two treatises

al-Tanwīr fī Isqāṭ al-Tadbīr — to lay down false control. Laṭāʾif al-Minan — the Shādhilī way as living transmission.
