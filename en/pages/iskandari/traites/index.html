The way of wisdom · Section 4

الكتابان

The two treatises

Alongside the Kitāb al-Ḥikam, Ibn ʿAṭāʾ Allāh leaves two books that prolong his voice: a treatise on tawakkul, and the living account of the Shādhilī way.

I

التنوير في إسقاط التدبير

al-Tanwīr fī Isqāṭ al-Tadbīr

The illumination found in relinquishing the false self-governance. Ibn ʿAṭāʾ Allāh’s great treatise on living trust: not passivity, but the withdrawal from the illusion that my anxiety fabricates reality. Do what depends on you — then surrender the outcome.

Treatise of tawakkul
II

لطائف المنن

Laṭāʾif al-Minan

The subtleties of the gifts concerning Abū al-ʿAbbās al-Mursī and his master Abū al-Ḥasan al-Shādhilī. The way as living companionship: the doctrine is read in gestures, words, presences. A book of transmission.

Account of the Shādhilī way

The two treatises are in Arabic, currently being integrated. For now, each page presents the axis of the book, its key modules, and the wisdoms of the Ḥikam that relate to it.
