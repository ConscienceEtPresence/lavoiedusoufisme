اَلْوَهَّاب
al-Wahhāb

The Bestower

He who gives without recompense or motive.

The tongue
From w-h-b; hiba is the pure gift — without exchange, without debt. An intensive form: He gives and gives again, without wearying.
The reason
The gift precedes merit: before you existed, you had nothing that would justify being given being. Everything since is of this order.
The Sufi meaning
To recognise this Name is to cease believing that one has earned anything. Gratitude is no longer mere courtesy: it becomes vertigo.
The servant's portion
Give without expecting — neither return, nor recognition, nor even to see yourself give.
