اَلرَّقِيب
ar-Raqīb

The Watchful

Present to all, an attentive witness.

The tongue
From r-q-b; raqaba is the nape of the neck — hence to watch over: the one who stands behind you, who sees the back of your neck.
The reason
No act, no thought unfolds without His attentive presence.
The Sufi meaning
To live in the sense of ar-Raqīb is murāqaba — inner vigilance: making of each act an act done before Him, hence in full presence.
The servant's portion
Watch over yourself as you would watch over a child: not to punish, but so as not to lose sight of it.
