اَلْوَدُود
al-Wadūd

The Loving

Who loves and lets Himself be loved.

The tongue
From w-d-d; wudd is love-tenderness, lasting affection. A faʿūl (فَعُول) form that can be active (He loves) and passive (He is loved).
The reason
He loves His creatures before they love Him; it is His love that calls forth theirs in return.
The Sufi meaning
Al-Wadūd is the secret Name of the Sufis: God is not only to be feared, He is to be loved. The whole way lies in this shift.
The servant's portion
Love, simply. Love those who love you, love those who do not love you. And know that, first of all, you are loved.
